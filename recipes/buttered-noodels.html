Buttered Noodles

Description:

A simple pasta dish consisting of the noodles of your choice, satueed in a butter sauce and topped with parmersean cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 tb-spoon kosher salt
  • 8 oz Noodles
  • 2 tb-spoon butter
  • 2 tb-spoon grated parmesean cheese
  • 1 tb-spoon chopped parsely
  • ground black pepper - to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a medium saucepan of water to a boil.
  2. Add and cook the pasta until desire doneness.
  3. Add the butter, parsely, and cheese. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
